Safety Issues

Top 10 FAQ

Q: How does Face 2 Face show people near me?

A: F2F uses your device's GPS (with your permission) to show a live map of nearby users. Your exact location is fuzzy (200m radius) for safety.

Q: Can someone see my exact location?

A: No. We never share your exact GPS coordinates. Others see an approximate area only. You can go invisible at any time.

Q: What's a Bump?

A: A Bump is a friendly "hey, I'm interested!" signal. If the other person Bumps you back, you can start chatting.

Q: Is Face 2 Face free?

A: Yes! Core features (map, chat, Bumps) are free forever. Premium adds extended range, profile boosts, and advanced filters.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Settings → Account → Delete Account. All data is permanently erased within 72 hours per Mexico's LFPDPPP.
