## 3. COUNTER-ARGUMENTS

### "Isn't this just another dating app?"
"Face 2 Face isn't a dating app — it's a social utility with three pillars. Dating is one-third. Think of us as a live map of your social world."

### "What about safety?"
"Safety is our biggest advantage. On traditional apps, you're talking to a photo that could be fake — $1.3B in scams proves that. On F2F, you see real people in real places. You meet in public, surrounded by others."

### "How is this different from Happn?"
"Happn shows you people you already crossed paths with — historical GPS. F2F shows a live map of who's available right now. We also go beyond dating with three pillars, and we eliminate all matching mechanics."
