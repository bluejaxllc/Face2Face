## 5. PRE-SEED / SEED PLANNING

### Round Sizes (2024-2025)
| Stage | Raise | Post-Money | Instrument |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-Seed | $750K-$1.5M | $4M-$6M | Post-Money SAFE |
| Seed | $2.5M-$3.5M | $12M-$15M | SAFE / Priced Equity |

### Equity Dilution
| Stage | Dilution | Cumulative Founder Ownership |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-Seed | 10-20% | ~80-90% |
| Seed | 15-25% | ~60-75% |
| Series A | 15-25% | ~45-60% |

### Key SAFE Terms to Negotiate
- Valuation Cap (higher = less dilution)
- Discount Rate (typically 20%)
- Pro Rata Rights (limit to major investors)
- "Lower Of" Rule (understand before signing)

## RECOMMENDED FUNDING ROADMAP

### Phase 1: Bootstrap (Now → Month 6-12)
- Keep burn <$2K/mo (Supabase free tier)
- Apply for AWS Activate ($1K-$100K credits), GCP ($200K-$350K)
- Launch in 1-2 Mexican cities → 1K-5K users
- Apply to Start-Up Chile Ignite ($15K-$40K equity-free)

### Phase 2: Grants + Angels (Month 6-18)
- NSF SBIR Phase I ($305K non-dilutive)
- Angel round ($100K-$300K) via AngelList
- Apply to YC or MassChallenge
- Target: 10K+ MAU, 25%+ D30 retention

### Phase 3: Pre-Seed (Month 12-24)
- Raise $750K-$1.5M via SAFE at $4M-$6M cap
- Target: a16z Speedrun, Antler, Precursor, LATAM funds
- Optional: Republic crowdfunding ($200K-$500K)

### Phase 4: Seed (Month 18-30)
- Raise $2.5M-$3.5M at $12M-$15M
- Requirements: $50K-$200K ARR, 10K+ active users
- Begin US expansion
