🧠 User Psychology & Behavioral Design

The behavioral science principles powering every F2F design decision

🧲 Proximity Bias

People are 3x more likely to form relationships with those physically nearby (Festinger, 1950). F2F weaponizes this with the live map — showing real people in real proximity creates urgency that no algorithm can manufacture.

⏰ FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

The live map creates natural FOMO: "There are 12 people near me RIGHT NOW and I might miss a connection." Unlike Tinder where profiles persist forever, F2F pins are ephemeral — they disappear when users leave.

🤝 Reciprocity Principle

The Bump system triggers reciprocity: when someone Bumps you, you feel socially obligated to respond. This creates a 2-3x higher response rate vs cold messaging (Cialdini, 1984).

🏆 Variable Reward Schedule

Opening the map is a variable reward: sometimes 15 people are nearby, sometimes 3. This unpredictability is the same mechanism that makes slot machines addictive — but we channel it toward real human connection.

👥 Social Proof

Seeing other pins on the map IS social proof. "If 20 people are using this app at this café, it must be worth using." Each new user literally makes the map more valuable for everyone.

🎯 Endowed Progress Effect

Gamification streaks and XP leverage the endowed progress effect — once users have a 7-day streak, they'll fight to keep it. Losing progress feels 2x worse than gaining it (Prospect Theory).

⚠️ Anti-Dark-Pattern Commitment

F2F explicitly rejects: infinite scroll, like counts as vanity metrics, hidden unsubscribe flows, and notification spam. We use psychology to connect people, not to extract attention. Our success metric is meetups, not screen time.

⚠️ Critical Migration Needed

Photos: Currently stored as base64 in the users.photo column. A single 2MB photo = 2.67MB base64 string in the DB. At 10K users, that's 26GB of photo data in PostgreSQL. Must migrate to S3/R2 object storage.

D1/D7/D30 strategies, churn prevention, and re-engagement campaigns

📱 Day 0-1: Activation

  • Hook: First map view must show 3+ pins (pre-seed if needed)
  • Action: Guide to first Bump within 2 minutes
  • Push: If app closed before first Bump → D1 morning push: "5 people are near you right now"
  • Reward: First Bump = +25 XP, celebratory animation

🔥 Day 2-7: Habit Formation

  • Streak: "You've used F2F 3 days in a row! 🔥" at D3
  • Social proof: "42 people joined F2F in your area this week"
  • Achievement: "Explorer" badge for visiting 3 different areas
  • Re-engagement: If no open by D4 → "Your Bump was accepted! Come chat"

💎 Day 8-30: Value Lock-In

  • Network effect: By D14, user should have 3+ ongoing conversations
  • Investment: Profile customization, game scores, XP = switching cost
  • Events: Push for first F2F-facilitated real-world meetup
  • Premium tease: D14 → "Try Premium free for 3 days"

🚨 Churn Prevention Signals

No opens in 3 days→ Push: "Someone new is near your favorite spot"
No Bumps in 7 days→ Email: Weekly activity summary + highlights
No opens in 14 days→ "We miss you" + new feature announcement
No opens in 30 days→ Final email. If no return → mark dormant, stop all comms
