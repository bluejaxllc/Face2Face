🎯 Core Thesis

Face 2 Face is the antidote to the dating app. It restores traditional rules of engagement — walking up to someone, shaking hands, speaking face to face — in a digital format. No swipes. No paywalls. No algorithmic gatekeeping. Just a live map of real people around you, and the freedom to approach them directly.

⚡ Competitive Edge

  • Three Pillars: Social, Business, and Love — not just dating
  • Open Communication: No match-gating. Direct interaction
  • Live Map: Real-time geographic proximity, not algorithmic feeds
  • "Pass By": Graceful rejection without confrontation
  • Group Function: Squad-based virality loop

User Acquisition Strategy

Three-phase go-to-market anchored in real-world utility and viral squad dynamics

💡 Primary Selling Proposition

Position Face 2 Face as the ultimate utility app for genuine, real-world interactions across all pillars of life: Social, Business, and Love. It counters algorithmic manipulation and digital isolation with geographic, organic connection.

Phase 1

Guerrilla Festival & Event Activations

By targeting high-density, real-world events, F2F immediately demonstrates its core location-centric utility.

🔥 Burning Man & Transformational Festivals

Emphasize the app as a "camp finder" and organic connection tool. The off-the-grid, communal mentality aligns with minimizing screen time and maximizing face-to-face interaction.

🏰 Renaissance Festivals & Niche Conventions

Tap into interactive, tightly-knit communities that prefer face-to-face interaction and organic community building.

🤝 Trade Shows & Networking Events

Leverage the Business module for instant deal-making and LinkedIn-style networking. F2F becomes a digital business card on the exhibition floor.

Phase 2

Group Function for Virality

Instead of acquiring users one-by-one, F2F acquires friend groups simultaneously — significantly lowering user acquisition costs.

🦸 Wingman/Wingwoman Functionality

A social safety net where friends share locations, vet matches together, and facilitate group meetups.

👥 Squad Dynamics

Market the utility of finding other groups: "Two friends looking for another duo at this festival." This lowers the intimidation factor, replacing it with low-pressure socializing.

Phase 3

Influencer Partnerships & Outreach

Convert rational, mature influencers (therapists, psychologists, authentic coaches) into deeply invested brand ambassadors.

🧪 Product Development Input

Offer early beta access and solicit professional feedback on app mechanics. Give them a voice to solve the dating issues they critique daily.

💊 "Antidote" Positioning

Position F2F as the "antidote to the dating app" — reducing swipe fatigue, emphasizing face-to-face communication, preventing ghosting, and destroying the Match Group monopoly.

📖 The POF Blueprint

Markus Frind built Plenty of Fish as a solo founder. He managed the entire site from his Vancouver apartment for 5 years with zero employees. By 2008 when he finally began hiring, the site had grown to 15M users generating $10M annual revenue — all from Google AdSense on a completely free platform. He maintained 100% equity and sold to Match Group in 2015 for $575M in cash.
