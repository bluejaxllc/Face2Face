Section 1: Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when creating an account, such as your phone number, email address, and profile details including photographs and biography. This helps create an authentic environment for face-to-face interaction.

Automatically collected information includes device identifiers, browser types, and usage data via cookies to improve the seamless delivery of the Face 2 Face experience.

Section 2: How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes matching you with potential connections based on shared interests, sending service notifications, and ensuring the safety and security of our community.

my_location

Section 3: Location Data

To facilitate real-time connections, we process your location data in real-time. Crucially, Face 2 Face does not store your location history. Your precise coordinates are used momentarily to identify nearby users and are discarded immediately after the session ends.

Section 4: Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with service providers who help us operate our platform, or when required by law to protect the rights and safety of our users.

Section 5: Data Security

Security is at the heart of Deep Space Intimacy. We use industry-standard End-to-End (E2E) encryption for all messaging and private data transmission. Our servers are housed in Tier-4 data centers with 24/7 monitoring to prevent unauthorized access.

Section 6: Your Rights

Under global data protection laws, you maintain the following rights over your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correct: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Delete: Request the permanent removal of your account and data.
  • Right to Export: Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format.

Section 7: Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. Inactive accounts are flagged after 12 months, and associated personal data is purged within 30 days of a deletion request.

Section 8: Third-Party Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and recommend reviewing their policies separately.

Section 9: Children's Privacy

Face 2 Face is strictly for individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we become aware of such data, it will be immediately deleted.

Section 10: International Users

By using the service, you consent to the processing of your information in the United States, where our primary servers are located. We adhere to Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international data transfers.

Section 11: Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Face 2 Face constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Section 12: Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy at Face 2 Face, please reach out to our dedicated privacy officer.
