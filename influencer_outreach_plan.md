# Influencer Outreach Plan & Scripts

**Objective:**
Partner with mature, rational professionals (clinical psychologists, relationship coaches, behavioral scientists) to establish Face 2 Face as a healthy, utility-driven alternative to the toxic, algorithm-based dating app monopoly.

**Target Personas:**
- Clinical Psychologists (e.g., Dr. Nicole LePera, Dr. Alexandra Solomon)
- Relationship Coaches (e.g., Jillian Turecki, Matthew Hussey)
- Behavioral Scientists (e.g., Logan Ury)

---

## The Pitch Strategy

**The Hook:** Dating apps are actively damaging mental health. You talk about this every day. We built the antidote.
**The Ask:** We want your raw feedback on our platform, and if you believe in it, we want you to be an early voice advocating for a return to real-world interactions.
**The Tone:** Respectful, professional, anti-gimmick. No "dating hacks" or "pickup artists".

---

## Outreach Templates

### Template 1: The Direct Professional Approach (For Apex/Tier 1)
**Subject:** The antidote to dating app fatigue (Feedback Request)

Hi [Name],

I follow your content closely, especially your insights on [Insert specific topic they recently covered, e.g., how algorithmic swiping creates a paradox of choice]. 

We entirely agree. In fact, we believe the current dating app model is fundamentally broken—designed for retention rather than connection. 

We are launching **Face 2 Face**, an "anti-dating app." There are no algorithms, no swiping, and no paywalls. It’s simply a live proximity grid that shows who is around you in real-time, encouraging people to look up from their phones and walk up to each other in the real world. We've replaced the anxiety of "read receipts" with the natural grace of a polite, in-person interaction.

Given your expertise in modern relational health, your feedback would be invaluable. We are not asking for a promotion—we are asking if you’d be willing to briefly review our platform thesis and tell us if we are on the right track in solving the digital isolation epidemic.

Would you be open to a 10-minute preview call or reviewing a brief executive summary?

Best regards,

[Your Name]
Founder, Face 2 Face

### Template 2: The "Burnout" Angle (For Coaches & Podcasts)
**Subject:** Stop Swiping. Start Walking Up. (Face 2 Face)

Hi [Name],

A recurring theme in your recent [posts/podcasts] is how burnt out everyone is from dating apps. The gamification, the ghosting, the endless swiping—it's exhausting your audience.

We decided to build the exact opposite. 

**Face 2 Face** eliminates the algorithm. It functions strictly as a real-world utility—like a radar for social life. You open it, see who is at the same coffee shop, festival, or bar, and you go talk to them. Direct, honest, and grounded in reality.

We’re putting together a coalition of relationship voices who want to champion a return to real life. We respect your voice in this space and want to show you what we're building. 

Are you open to connecting later this week to see how Face 2 Face aligns with your message?

Best,

[Your Name]
Founder, Face 2 Face

## Follow-up Protocol
- **Day 1:** Send Initial Email (using templates above).
- **Day 4:** First Follow-up (Reply in thread). *Focus: "I know you're busy. Just wanted to bump this. Even a 2 sentence reply with your initial thought on the 'no-algorithm' approach is incredibly valuable to us."*
- **Day 10:** Second Follow-up via LinkedIn or Instagram DM (if appropriate). *Focus: "Sent you an email recently about an anti-dating app project. Would love your eyes on it."*
- **Day 21:** Final Check-in. *Focus: Share a small, massive update (e.g., "We just crossed X beta users who all cited app burnout").*

## Compensation & Partnership Models
If they are interested in partnering after reviewing the app, propose these structures:
1. **The Advisory Route (Apex/Tier 1):** Offer a small equity advisory grant in exchange for public association and quarterly product feedback via their lens of psychological health.
2. **The Content Partnership (Tier 2):** Paid sponsorships for dedicated podcast integrations or Reels where they dissect *why* traditional apps cause anxiety and how the F2F model solves it.
