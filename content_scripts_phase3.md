# Phase 3: "Anti-Dating App" Content Scripts
**Theme:** Peak algorithmic fatigue, spoofing the Match Group monopoly, and highlighting the superiority of walking up to someone in real life.
**Formats:** TikToks, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

## Skit 1: The "Algorithm" Boardroom
**Tone:** Satirical, "The Office" style documentary.
**Characters:** 
- "The CEO" (Match Group proxy, slick, villainous but tries to seem caring).
- "Data Scientist Dave" (Nerdy, strictly about the numbers).

**(Scene: Dimly lit boardroom, graphs showing 'Time on App' going up).**
**CEO:** "Alright team, great quarter. Revenue is up 40%. But Dave, I'm seeing a problem. Two users... *met*. And they *deleted* the app."
**Dave (panicking):** "Sir, it was an anomaly! They bypassed the paywall, the algorithm accidentally showed them someone they actually liked in their city!"
**CEO:** "Dave. We are in the business of *keeping them swiping*, not *getting them married*. If they meet, they stop paying $30 a month for 'Super Likes'. Fix the algorithm. Show them people 500 miles away. Show them bots."
**Dave:** "Yes sir. Throttling matches now."
*Cut to black.*
**Voiceover (or Text on Screen):** "They don't want you to meet. They want you addicted. Break the cycle. Just walk up to them. **Face 2 Face**."

## Skit 2: The "Read Receipt" Anxiety
**Tone:** Relatable, hyper-realistic anxiety.
**Characters:** 
- "Sarah" (Staring intensely at her phone).
- "Inner Monologue" (Voiceover).

**(Scene: Sarah is in her bedroom, looking at a chat interface that looks like Tinder/Hinge).**
**Inner Monologue:** "Okay, he read it at 9:02 PM. It's 9:06 PM. Why hasn't he replied? Is he typing? No dots. Did I use too many emojis? Maybe I shouldn't have said 'hey'. Oh god, he's active now. Still no reply."
*(Sarah throws her phone on the bed, stressed).*
*Cut to:*
**(Scene: Sarah is at a coffee shop. A guy walks past her, she smiles, he smiles back. He stops).**
**Guy:** "Hey, I love your jacket."
**Sarah (smiling immediately):** "Thanks!"
*Cut to black.*
**Voiceover/Text:** "Zero read receipts. Zero overthinking. 100% direct interaction. Return to reality. **Face 2 Face**."

## Skit 3: The Profile Translator
**Tone:** Sarcastic, educational.
**Characters:** "The Translator" (Looking directly at camera, holding a pointer).

**(Scene: The Translator standing in front of a green screen showing generic dating app screenshots).**
**Translator:** "Welcome to Dating App Translation 101. 
When their bio says 'Fluent in sarcasm', translation: 'I will be mean to you and pass it off as a joke.'
When their bio says 'Looking for a partner in crime', translation: 'I have literally no original thoughts.'
And when the app says 'You have 99+ likes! Upgrade to see them!', translation: 'We are holding your self-esteem hostage for $14.99 a week.'
Stop treating people like resumes. Go outside. Look around. **Face 2 Face**."

## Skit 4: The Vetting Meeting vs. The Interaction
**Tone:** Absurdist contrast.
**(Scene A: Split screen or fast cuts).**
**Guy 1 (on phone):** "Okay, she's a Virgo, she likes hiking, she hates pineapple on pizza. I must craft the perfect opening line referencing the dog in her 4th picture." *(Starts typing furiously, backspacing, sweating).*
**(Scene B: In real life, at a bar).**
**Guy 2 (sees a girl, walks up):** "Hi, I'm Mike."
**Girl:** "Hi, I'm Jenna."
*(Both smile).*
*Cut to black.*
**Voiceover:** "Stop interviewing. Start interacting. The algorithm is you. **Face 2 Face**."
